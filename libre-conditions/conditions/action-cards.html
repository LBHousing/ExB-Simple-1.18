Report a Violation

If you're living with mold, pests, broken plumbing, or other dangerous conditions, you have the right to report them. Landlord retaliation for reporting is illegal under California law.

Contact Long Beach Code Enforcement: 562-570-2633

Know Your Eviction Rights

If you receive an eviction notice, do not move out immediately. You have legal rights and the eviction process takes 6-8 weeks. File an Answer within 10 court days.

Free legal help: Stay Housed LA · LAFLA · LiBRE workshops
